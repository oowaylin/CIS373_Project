Geography

Myanmar's geography varies with five distinct physiographic regions: the northern mountains, the western ranges, the eastern plateu, the central basin and lowlands, and the coastal plains.

Ethnic Groups & Languages

There are five major ethnic groups: Burmans, Karen, Shan, Irrawaddy, and Sittang.The official language of the country is Burmese, though this is segmented into different dialects.

Economy

The national currency of is called kyat, and most of the internal economy is based off aggriculture. Of the large industrial enterprises, the country trades mostly in produce, forestry and fishing.

Government

Since the signing of the first constitution in 1974 there has been 4 major shifts in power, 1988, 1997, 2011, and 2020. The government is largely controlled by its military leaders.

Culture

Culture in Myanmar has come mainly buddhist beliefs mixed with local non-buddhist beliefs. There was a monarchy that was estbalished in the 19th century that hold the responsibility for establishing the country's notable pagoda structures.

History

The country's geographical location has been significant for trade. This importance has made the location a historical mixing ground for merchants and cultures. Many foreign countries have their merchants trade through routes found in Myanmar. The land has always been sought after.
